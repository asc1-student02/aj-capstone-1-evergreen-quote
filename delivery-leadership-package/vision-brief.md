## Who is the customer?
A first-time insurance shopper — typically a new renter or homeowner — who needs
a quick, no-commitment price estimate on their phone. Today they either leave the
site when asked for too much information, or they call an agent just to get a number.

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
Shoppers are forced to fill out long forms and hand over their email before seeing
any price. Most leave before they get a quote. Evergreen removes that wall — no
account, no email, just a fast monthly number so the customer can decide if it's
worth exploring further.
